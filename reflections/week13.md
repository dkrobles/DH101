# Week 13 Reflection

Reflect 13: Do we imagine AI futures as utopian, dystopian, or something in between? What do those visions reveal about us?

I believe the future of AI will be very in-between, messy, and dependent upon the decisions of people rather than on the AI itself. Some of the future scenarios involve increasing opportunities for education, healthcare, and artistic creations, whereas others imply worsening inequality, increased surveillance, and increased dependence on institutions.

All these possible realities tell us a lot about ourselves now. Optimistic AI worlds represent our desire for equality, effective work, and development; the. dark AI future shows our fears that we may lose control, our right to privacy, and even our purpose in life.

In fact, the future world built by AI is just a projection of the current reality with its economic and political inequality and lack of confidence in institutions. What is important is that the role of AI is only to amplify the existing social realities.
