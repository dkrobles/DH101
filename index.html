Welcome

This site documents my critical making journey through DH101. Each week, I engage in hands-on projects that explore the intersection of technology, culture, and creativity. Through reverse engineering, prototyping, and critical analysis, I examine what is being made, what data powers these creations, and what implications arise from our digital tools and systems.
