# How I Use AI

Describe how you approach AI use in this course.

“How I Use AI” page

Tools Used: ChatGPT(brainstorming + editing)

What I Asked AI To Do:
Generate game ideas about human-AI relationships
Help structure branching narrative
Edit and refine writing

What I Kept:
Overall structure of the interview concept
Some dialogue ideas

What I Changed:
Rewrote most dialogue in my own voice
Adjusted choices to better reflect my theme
Simplified the branching structure

What I Discarded:
Overly complex storylines
Dialogue that felt too robotic or unrealistic

My AI Use Policy:
I used AI as a support tool, not a replacement for my creativity. I made final decisions about story, structure, and meaning.
