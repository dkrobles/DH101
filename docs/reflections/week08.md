# Week 8 Reflection

Reflect 8: Who is visible/invisible in AI networks? How does visualization reveal or obscure power?

In AI networks, some are more visible, while others are mostly invisible. For example, tech companies, corporate headquarters, and AI researchers are often visible due to the attention and funding they receive for innovation. However, most people who make AI networks possible are invisible. For example, people who work as data labelers, content moderators, miners of rare earth materials, and people who work in the physical spaces are mostly invisible. Power can be visualized in AI networks by showing where the resources are, where the servers are, and where the corporate headquarters are, often in rich countries or tech centers, However, the visualization of AI networks can be problematic in showing the power dynamics of the networks. For example, the networks can be too simplistic and omit the people and the environment that make the networks possible.
