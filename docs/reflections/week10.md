# Week 10 Reflection

Reflect 10: How does AI change our relationship to play, rules, and narrative?

The influence of AI is reflected in its alteration of the ways we engage with play, rules, and stories. Unlike the static rules that define traditional games, artificial intelligence allows one to alter them based own the actions of the player and his or her skill level in real-time. As a result, playing with AI gives us greater opportunities to explore the game world freely and try something new. At the same time, AI allows stories to unfold in various ways, creating different dialogues and leading to unexpected conclusions.
