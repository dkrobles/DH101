# Week 9 Reflection

Reflect 9: What is creativity when AI can generate endlessly? Where is the human in generative work?

Creativity, even in the age of AI-generated content, becomes more about intention, selection, and meaning, rather than content creation. The human is again central, but now as curator, defining what is worthy of preservation, refinement, and sharing. As a creator of the bot, my experience was that, while AI can produce novel and diverse content, it is heavily dependent on how I framed my inputs. I was not in complete control of my outputs but was, in fact, working alongside my AI, making adjustments to my inputs to elicit better outputs. It was not so much about controlling my AI but more about guiding a conversation, in which human judgement was always going to be what defined valuable or creative output.
