# Week 3 Reflection

Reflect 3: What does “authenticity” mean when identity is co-constructed with AI?

When identity is co-constructed alongside AI, authenticity entails being aware of the difference between things that come from your and things that come from AI. Sometimes, the role of AI is to create the way you express yourself, to suggest ideas, language, or points of view that you might not have thought of yourself. The problem, however, is that you have to ask who is in control. To be authentic is to mean that you are intentional and in control, and not defined by AI. Your inauthenticity or authenticity is not diminished or changed just because it is co-constructed alongside AI but is transformed into something that offers you the power to reflect, to choose, and to take ownership of who you are.
