# Week 12 Reflection

Reflect 12: Is AI sustainable? What ecological trade-offs are we willing to accept?

Even though AI isn't sustainable yet, it could be made more so if approached responsibly when it comes to its design and regulation. In order to train large machine learning models, enormous amounts of electricity and cooling water along with rare earth elements need to be utilized, which in turn produces greenhouse gases and other negative environmental effects. On the other hand, it is clear that AI can help optimize the energy consumption process, model climate change, and manage our natural resources more efficiently. It all boils down to the question of striking the right balance between innovation and convenience, on one hand, and the price it will take from nature, on the other.
